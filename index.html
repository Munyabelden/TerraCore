Who We Are

Founded in 2005, TerraCore Dynamics has grown from a local Cape Town provider to a national leader in heavy machinery outsourcing for the South African mining industry. We understand that in mining, downtime is not an option. Our mission is to provide unparalleled reliability, cutting-edge equipment, and expert support to keep your operations running at peak efficiency.


We bridge the gap between operational needs and capital expenditure, offering flexible, scalable solutions that empower our clients to tackle ambitious projects, from deep-pit excavation to large-scale earth-moving, with confidence and precision.

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for every operational challenge.

Equipment Leasing

Access a state-of-the-art fleet of haul trucks, excavators, dozers, and loaders without the capital outlay. Flexible short-term and long-term contracts.

On-Site Maintenance

Our 24/7/365 expert mechanical teams integrate with your operation, providing preventative maintenance and rapid-response repairs to maximize uptime.

National Logistics

We manage the complex logistics of mobilizing and demobilizing heavy-scale equipment to remote and challenging sites across South Africa.

Operator Training

Certified training programs for advanced machinery operation, safety protocols, and simulator-based training to ensure your team is safe and efficient.
