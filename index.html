Who We Are

GS Commodities is a premier South African supplier dedicated to efficiency and reliability. We specialize in two core areas: the general trading of everyday industrial commodities and the specialized sourcing of high-grade electrical and mining equipment.


While we hold a primary contract with a major strategic partner, our capacity allows us to supply clients across the Western Cape and **worldwide**. Whatever the requirement—a specific product or a full service—**we will source and get it to you, wherever you are.**
